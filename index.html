What is the Diabetes Risk Screener?

The Gateway to Early Detection

  • Digital tool (Loop): QR code in-clinic or WhatsApp link.
  • Validated criteria: BMI, waist circumference, lifestyle, family history, blood pressure, glucose history.
    • Medium/High risk → prompted to check HbA1c or join bundle.
    • Low risk → encouraged to retest annually & join healthy habit challenges.

How Customers Enter the Program

Enrollment Flows for New & Existing Customers

New Customers

Flow A (Screener → MCU → Bundle): Start with QR/WhatsApp screener → High risk gets HbA1c/bundle → MCU confirms → Enroll in DPP/DMP.

Flow B (Direct MCU → Bundle): Customer does MCU → Results show prediabetes/diabetes → Enroll in the appropriate bundle (DPP or DMP).

Existing Customers

Flow C (Data Outreach → Bundle): Identified via past data → CS outreach invites to bundle.

Flow D (Direct Enrollment): Known risk/diabetes → Joins appropriate Care Bundle (DPP or DMP) directly.

For All Customers

Loop provides daily coaching & engagement via app, while DiagnosLab handles quarterly MCU follow-ups (HbA1c ± metabolic panel).

Our Care Programs & Bundles

Two Programs, Offered in Flexible Bundles

Preventive Health (DPP)

For at-risk or prediabetes.

Base Bundle (6 or 12 months)

Loop: Daily coaching, lifestyle tracking, monthly coach calls.

DiagnosLab: Quarterly HbA1c test.

Prevention Care Plus

Loop: Same as Base.

DiagnosLab: Full metabolic panel (lipids, kidney, liver) + BP, BMI, waist circumference every 3 months.

Diabetes Care (DMP)

For confirmed diabetes (HbA1c ≥ 6.5% or FPG ≥ 126 mg/dL).

Base Bundle (6 or 12 months)

Loop: Structured care, unlimited coach messaging, daily engagement.

DiagnosLab: Quarterly HbA1c test.

Diabetes Care Plus

Loop: Same as Base + personalized nutrition guidance.

DiagnosLab: Full metabolic panel + BP, BMI, waist circumference every 3 months.

Other Bundles

Corporate Wellness Bundle: Loop (Risk calculator, health talks, coaching, reporting) + DiagnosLab (On-site screenings, annual MCU, HbA1c testing).
Precision Health Bundle (Future): Loop (Genomic-based coaching integration) + DiagnosLab (Genomics testing & annual re-testing).
