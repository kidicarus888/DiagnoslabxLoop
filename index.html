In-Clinic

Comprehensive Bundles for Proactive Health

A. Prevention Package

Target: Individuals at risk of prediabetes.

  • 1x Foundational MCU Blood Test:
    • • HbA1c: An average of your blood sugar over the past 2-3 months, key for prediabetes screening.
  • 3-month Loop Digital Prevention Program
  • Personalized Lifestyle Coaching
  • Weight Management & Daily Engagement

B. Diabetes Care Package

Target: Individuals with confirmed diabetes (HbA1c ≥ 6.5%).

  • 3/6/12-Month Programs with scheduled HbA1c tests
  • Optional Comprehensive Metabolic Care:
    • Lipid Profile: TC, LDL, HDL, TG
    • Kidney Function: Creatinine, eGFR, Urine Albumin
    • Liver Function: AST/ALT
    • BP, BMI & Waist Circumference Tracking

C. Corporate Wellness Package

Target: Employers focused on employee health.

  • On-site Employee Screenings
  • Free Calculator Access for all staff
  • Risk-based Program Enrollment (DPP/DMP)
  • "Diabetes 101" Health Talk by Loop
  • Monthly Outcome Reporting
  • Optional Annual Follow-ups & Workshops

D. Precision Health Package (Future)

Target: Proactive individuals & corporates.

  • 1x Genomics Test (Metabolic Predisposition)
  • 12-month Loop Program tailored to Genetic Profile
  • Lifestyle coaching based on genomics + clinical data
  • Annual Re-testing with DiagnosLab

Comprehensive KPI Framework for Success

Tracking key metrics across the entire funnel to ensure partnership success and tangible health outcomes.
